What is Sg2015?

To commemorate this significant milestone in our nation's history, we have taken the initiative to begin a ground-up movement that
aspires to create 50 people-driven project ideas based on the values in our National Pledge. We hope these projects can be
presented as a birthday gift to our nation on 9 Aug 2015.

Singapore belongs to us, her citizens, and we hope that SG2015 will bring us together as a nation,
bound in our shared purpose to define Singapore for the next 50 years.

ANNOUNCEMENT : Please visit www.sg2015.com from 1 Aug to upload your project ideas! In the meantime, do register with us if you are interested to be part of our first SG2015 volunteers' meeting scheduled for Aug 2013!
