Congratulation to taking your first step in presenting your gift to Singapore! We welcome any projects that fulfil the pledge according to the following eight categories:

We the Citizens - Projects that give Singapore a sense of family unity, that our island is more a home, than a hotel
Pledge ourselves as one united people - Projects that help Singapore living here or overseas feel a common sense of history, identity and destiny.
Regardless of Race, Language or Religion - Projects that increase inclusiveness without diminishing our unique background values or culture.
To build a democratic society - Projects that use technology or other innovation to harness the opinions and voices of people.
Based on Justice and Equality - Projects that help care for the forgotten or neglected
To achieve happiness - Projects that create a positive and resilient mindset
Prosperity - Projects that create wealth for individual residents in Singapore, especially if they are financially vulnerable
Progress for our Nation - Projects that protect our future in terms of the environment, culture and/or our comparative and competitive advantage.
